Mondays

Free Pool

Every Monday, Slammin Sams will be offering free pool to everyone of all ages. This offer is available every Monday from 4:00pm til close.

Tuesdays

Pool Tournament & $1 Draft

Every Tuesday, Slammin Sams will hold a pool tournament for people of all ages. The tournament will start at 8:00pm. There is a $5.00 entry fee, and the house will match the pot. We will be playing 8-Ball, and will be the best 2 out of 3. Also, every winner of each tournament will be automatically qualified for the Summer Classic pool tournament we will be holding in 16 weeks.

Dont forget to ask your bartender or waitress about the Dollar Beer!

Catfish buffet & Karaoke

Every Wednesday, Slammin Sams is offering All You Can Eat catfish for only $10.00! If you like catfish, and you have ten dollars, come try this amazing deal out.

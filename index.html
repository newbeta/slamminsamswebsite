About Slammin' Sams

Slammin' Sams is the place to be for delicious food and the latest sports that you can view on the largest High-Def T.V.'s on the coast!. We have two sides from which you can choose, each having its own unique setting.

Be sure to check out the details of our Summer Classic pool tournament!

Casual Dining

Our Casual Dining side offers a No Smoking atmosphere with two High-Definition T.V's so you can catch the latest news and sports while you enjoy a nice, family dinner.

Sports Bar & Grill

Our Sports Bar & Grill side offers a full bar and 4 High-Definition T.V's to catch the latest sports events including all UFC Fights. You can also catch all the regular season and post-season games from your favorite sport. Whether you like college basketball or professional football, the Slammin Sams Sports Bar and Grill is where you need to be.
